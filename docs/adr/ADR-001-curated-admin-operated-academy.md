# ADR-001: Curated Admin-Operated Academy

**Status:** Accepted
**Date:** 2026-06-13

## Context

A marketplace would require mentor onboarding, permissions, content review, payouts, commissions, disputes, analytics, and self-service publishing. These systems do not help validate the first customer learning and payment journey.

## Decision

Yosr will launch as a curated, Admin-operated academy.

Admins create and publish Courses. Mentors are public content records only and are not authenticated platform users.

## Consequences

- The MVP has fewer roles and trust boundaries.
- Content quality and publication remain under internal control.
- Mentor content transfer is an external operational process.
- Mentor dashboards, payouts, commissions, and marketplace economics require a future product decision rather than incremental UI additions.
