# ADR-009: External Support Channels First

**Status:** Accepted
**Date:** 2026-06-13

## Context

Customers need accessible local support, but building live chat, ticketing, chatbot, and AI support inside the MVP would add substantial product and operational scope.

## Decision

Use WhatsApp and email as the MVP support channels. Store internal Operational Notes against relevant Yosr records when support context must be preserved.

Future support systems integrate through stable Customer, Order, Payment, Enrollment, Course, and trace references.

## Consequences

- Support can launch without an internal messaging product.
- Admin workflows must make records easy to find from safe public references.
- Future helpdesk, chat, or AI support remains possible.
- Support providers receive only the minimum required customer data.
